Why am I lazy?

My brain needs a break

I haven’t been very active on social media and I haven’t worked on my website for few weeks.

Not everything has stopped as I have been going to work and I have been motivated as usual. I have even continued going to the gym almost every morning. But it looks like some people are able to have a non-stop life all the time - or maybe do they just fake it?

I definitely cannot although I am not sure if it comes from the fact that I put too much into my daily work so I do not often have any energy left for anything else, or that I have a short attention span (I like to do different things) or maybe because I simply love the escape offered by watching TV series and movies. Probably a mix of everything?

However I have to admit that it is much harder to get back into the rhythm of coding and my blog after a long break.
When I work on my blog regularly, everything is there ready for me to start as soon as I open my laptop, I know exactly where to click, what I want and where I want to end. Starting after a long break takes time, opening up my code files, logging in to github, understanding my code and knowing what I want to focus on next. Note to myself: annotate my code better.

I truly believe that everyone ought to listen to their body and brain, something that I don’t do as often as I should, something that I try to do once a week at least when my yoga teacher reminds me that I should. So I am not sure if I become lazy suddenly because I don’t listen to those precursor signs or on the contrary perhaps I do listen subconsciously to them and consequently I take a break.

Next time I will try harder to analyse the ‘signs’ to try to answer the above. Maybe I need to increase my yoga sessions to have a ‘mind check’ more often ;)?
